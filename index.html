FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Here are the top questions our clients ask before getting started.

How long does a typical carbon footprint assessment take?

A standard assessment usually takes between 4 to 6 weeks, depending on the complexity of your supply chain and the availability of your internal data. We guide you through the collection process to make it as seamless as possible.

What industries do you work with?

We specialize in supporting SMEs across technology, manufacturing, retail, and professional services. Our methodologies adapt to the unique carbon emission scopes relevant to your specific sector.

Do we even have the internal capability to pull this off?

Absolutely. You don't need a dedicated sustainability team. We act as your external carbon department, doing the heavy lifting on calculation and strategy so your team can stay focused on core operations.
